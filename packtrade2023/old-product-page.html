Консультація
Опис:

A small-sized stone mill of up to 45 kg hourly capacity, able to satisfy the needs of a family, small bakery, and a coffee house in whole-grain flour. The advantage of this model is its lightweight and small dimensions.

Основні характеристики

Hourly capacity to 45 kg
Hopper capacity28 L
Millstone diameter300 mm / 11.81″
Millstone materialsolid hard-type granite stone
Body materialalder & walnut wood
External dimensionW 590 / D 798 / H 1155 mm
Weight100 kg
Engine1.5 kW / 3 Ph / from 220 V / 50 Hz

The mill is equipped with:

  • An emergency shutdown system in the case of grain absence;
  • A frequency converter: allows to work from 220 V, regulate the millstone rotation speed, decrease power usage and increase service life;
  • Mechanical regulation of the grain discharge;
  • A regulator of the millstone height;
  • A moisture ventilation hole.

Additionally:

  • Possibility to install a sifting machine.

Відео

Natural stone millstones

The Miller uses natural granite. The grain does not overheat, does not absorb toxic substances and retains its unique properties.

Ideal grain grinding

Flexible setting system and the manual mode

Presentable design

A mill is not only a useful tool but also a luxurious interior design item that will attract attention and matches the interior of a cafe, bakery, or domestic kitchen.

Worldwide shipping

The Miller mills are bought not only in Ukraine. We ship equipment all over the world.
