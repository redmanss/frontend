Опис:

A small-sized stone mill of up to 45 kg hourly capacity, able to satisfy the needs of a family, small bakery, and a coffee house in whole-grain flour. The advantage of this model is its lightweight and small dimensions. A small-sized stone mill of up to 45 kg hourly capacity, able to satisfy the needs of a family, small bakery, and a coffee house in whole-grain flour. The advantage of this model is its lightweight and small dimensions.

Основна інформація

Hourly capacity to 45 kg
Hopper capacity28 L
Millstone diameter300 mm / 11.81″
Millstone materialsolid hard-type granite stone
Body materialalder & walnut wood
External dimensionW 590 / D 798 / H 1155 mm
Weight100 kg
Engine1.5 kW / 3 Ph / from 220 V / 50 Hz

Переваги:

  • An emergency shutdown system in the case of grain absence;
  • A frequency converter: allows to work from 220 V, regulate the millstone rotation speed, decrease power usage and increase service life;
  • Mechanical regulation of the grain discharge;
  • A regulator of the millstone height;
  • A moisture ventilation hole.

Additionally:

  • Possibility to install a sifting machine.
